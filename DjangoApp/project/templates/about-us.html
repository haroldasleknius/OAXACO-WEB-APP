About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Oaxaca, a Mexican restaurant dedicated to bringing you the authentic flavors and vibrant culture of the beautiful region of Oaxaca. Our restaurant is named after this region as we believe it captures the essence of the unique culinary traditions and cultural heritage of Mexico.

Our team is made up of passionate food lovers who believe that great food is more than just sustenance; it's an experience that nourishes the soul. Our talented chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create bold and flavorful dishes that capture the essence of Oaxacan cuisine. We offer a diverse menu that includes classic Oaxacan dishes such as Mole, Tlayudas, Tamales, and deliciously prepared Tacos.

Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch or celebrating a special occasion with friends and family, we promise to deliver an unforgettable experience. We invite you to join us on a culinary journey to Oaxaca, where you can savor the rich flavors and immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of this beautiful region.

We Care About The Environment

At Oaxaca, we believe that we have a responsibility to protect and preserve our planet for future generations. That's why we are committed to minimizing our environmental impact in every way possible.

We understand that the food industry can have a significant impact on the environment, and we take this responsibility seriously. To reduce our carbon footprint, we prioritize sourcing ingredients locally to reduce transportation emissions. We work closely with local farmers and artisans to ensure that our ingredients are fresh, high-quality, and sustainably sourced.

We also strive to minimize our waste and use eco-friendly practices in our daily operations. Our team is committed to recycling, composting, and minimizing our water usage. We use environmentally friendly packaging for our to-go orders and strive to use reusable items in our restaurant wherever possible.

Our Locations

Oaxaca HQ is our flagship location and the heart of our restaurant. Our restaurant is located in the beautiful city of San José in the state of Oaxaca, Mexico. San José is a small town known for its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty. Our restaurant is located in the heart of the town, just a few minutes' walk from the central plaza.

At Oaxaca HQ, we offer a warm and inviting atmosphere where guests can experience the authentic flavors of Oaxacan cuisine. Our restaurant is beautifully designed with traditional Oaxacan elements, including colorful textiles, handcrafted pottery, and artwork from local artists.

In addition to our flagship location, we have plans to expand to other cities in Mexico and beyond. We are dedicated to sharing the unique flavors and vibrant culture of Oaxaca with people around the world. Our future locations will embody the same commitment to quality, sustainability, and community that our flagship location represents.

Whether you are visiting San José or one of our future locations, we invite you to join us for an unforgettable culinary journey to Oaxaca. Experience the rich flavors, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality that make Oaxacan cuisine truly special.
