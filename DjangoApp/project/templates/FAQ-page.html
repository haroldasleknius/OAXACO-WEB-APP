Oaxaca Frequently Asked Questions

Customers can view the range of dishes and foods we offer by clicking the Menu button in the navigation bar at the top of the page.

At Oaxaca, we value our customers' feedback and take all complaints seriously. If you have an issue you would like to bring to our attention, please visit our Complaint Form, where a dedicated member of our staff will promptly review and address your concerns within 24 hours.

The hours of operation for Oaxaca are:

  • Monday-Thursday: 11:00am-10:00pm
  • Friday-Saturday: 11:00am-11:00pm
  • Sunday: 10:00am-9:00pm

    • Please note that these hours may vary on holidays and special occasions. We recommend calling ahead or checking our website for any updates on our hours of operation.

    To learn more about Oaxaca you can visit our About Us page on our website to learn more about the history, mission and vision of the restaurant, and our commitment to providing high-quality food and service.

    To place an order, you can either visit our restaurant and order in-person, give us a call at 07263819283 to place a takeout or delivery order. You can also order online through our website, visit our Menu to discover a diverse selection of delicious food options that cater to every palate.

    To create a customer account, you can visit our Sign up page. This will usually involve providing personal information such as your first and last name, as well as choosing a username and password. If you already have an account you can visit our Login page to sign in!

    We support Visa, Mastercard, American Express, as well as alternative payment methods such as Stripe, Amazon Pay, Apple Pay, and PayPal. It is always best to check with the specific company or organization to confirm which card providers they support.
